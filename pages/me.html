About Me

crafting stories where sound meets memory

Brooklyn Gibbs

Creative Technologist | Music Enthusiast | Nostalgia Curator

Hi, I’m Brooklyn—a 24-year-old creative spirit who thrives on exploring the intersection of music, technology, and human connection. I’m endlessly curious about how songs shape memories and how we can use technology to bring those stories to life in unique, meaningful ways.

With a passion for music recommendations, machine learning, and storytelling, my work often feels like crafting a digital mixtape—combining emotion, data, and a touch of whimsy. Whether I’m analyzing audio features or dreaming up new ways to connect people through music, I approach every project with a blend of research, creativity, and heart.
