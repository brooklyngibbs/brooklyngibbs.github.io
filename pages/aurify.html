Aurify

where your photos find their soundtrack

About the App

Aurify is a creative app that transforms your photos into personalized playlists. Using OpenAI's advanced models, the app analyzes the mood, colors, and composition of your photos to determine the emotional and thematic elements. Then, with the Spotify API, Aurify generates a playlist that matches the vibe of your photo and makes it available directly on Spotify for listening and sharing.

Whether it’s a snapshot from a road trip or a candid moment with friends, Aurify brings your memories to life through music that reflects the story behind the image.

Technology Behind Aurify

Photo Analysis

OpenAI's image and language models analyze the uploaded photo, extracting details such as color palette, lighting, mood, and context to understand the image's emotional tone.

Playlist Creation

The Spotify API fetches songs based on the attributes identified by OpenAI, creating a playlist tailored to the vibe of your photo. This playlist can be listened to, shared, and saved in Spotify.

Seamless Integration

Aurify bridges advanced AI analysis and Spotify’s music platform, ensuring that every playlist is not only unique but also accessible for streaming on a platform you already love.

Potential Use Cases

  • Create soundtracks for your favorite memories
  • Generate mood-based playlists for events or moments
  • Discover new music inspired by the aesthetic of your photos
  • Share personalized playlists with friends and social networks
