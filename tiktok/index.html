About

internet.siren is a TikTok account I started in April 2026, posting niche websites and the corners of the indie web that don't show up in feeds. It's also a learning project — a way to sharpen my graphic design, video editing, and social media instincts in public.

The format is collage carousels of websites I find while actually surfing the web. I've leaned into a textured, mixed-media style on purpose. In a moment when AI is flooding the internet with clean illustration and smooth design, friction has become the differentiator. I'm betting mixed media and collage are the next big aesthetic trend.
