Brooklyn Gibbs

I'm a jack of all trades — I write, I design, I run campaigns, and I ship the product myself.

My edge isn't "designer who codes" or "engineer with taste." It's that I've run a real product end to end, built a real audience from zero, and have the technical chops to actually ship anything I pitch. I started in tech — a B.S. in Computer Science and a stint as a data engineer at Capital One — then went founder-mode.

I'm the CEO and co-founder of Whimbitz, a mobile gaming app I shipped on both iOS and Android, and the founder of Offline Crush, an independent media brand with 10,000+ subscribers grown with zero paid acquisition. Along the way I've turned a TikTok account into a case study, and I build speculative campaigns for the brands and artists I'd love to work with.

What ties it together is a feel for culture — calling trends before they land and being able to explain why they're about to. I translate that cultural feeling into a visual identity, then build the thing. The dream is creative direction: social-first work and music rollouts where the strategy, the aesthetic, and the build all come from one person.
