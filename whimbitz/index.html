About

A TikTok-style app for mini-games. Built end-to-end through:

  • conceiving the product and the strategy
  • designing the UX and the brand identity
  • shipping iOS in SwiftUI
  • shipping Android in Kotlin
  • building the agentic system that generates each game
  • building the mascot world (Whimbleton, Professor Whim, Lady Whimberly)
  • running growth and social

Product

A sample of games from the platform:

Fruit Slice

A swipe-to-slice arcade game set behind a grocery checkout. Cut the fruit before it lands.

Global Flag Master

A geography quiz: see the flag, pick the country. Part of an education direction we're testing.

Deep Sea Angler

A pixel-art fishing game with a coral-reef stage. Bait, reel, count the cash.

Chroma Dash

A color-match speed game on a synthwave neon-city skyline. Match the target before the clock runs out.

World

Three mascots. Each runs the social account for a corner of the app.

Whimbleton

Chartreuse. Runs the arcade games account.

Professor Whim

Blue. Runs the educational games account.

Lady Whimberly

Pink. Runs the go-girl games account.
