About

A TikTok-style app for AI-generated mini-games. Built end-to-end through:

  • conceiving the product and the strategy
  • designing the UX and the brand identity
  • shipping iOS in SwiftUI
  • shipping Android in Kotlin
  • building the agentic system that generates each game
  • building the mascot world (Whimbleton, Professor Whim, Lady Whimberly)
  • running growth and social

Decisions

Why a TikTok-style format?

App Store discovery rewards brands you already know. A TikTok-style feed surfaces variety without that gravity — the format that works for short videos should work for short games.

Why an agent instead of a single LLM call?

A single LLM call generates a one-shot game. An agentic chain coordinates concept, art direction, and gameplay rules — letting each game feel deliberate instead of random. The variety in the Product section above is what that buys.

Why three mascots instead of one voice?

Players who want a quick arcade slice aren't the players who want to learn flags. Three mascots — each running their own social account — let each audience find a voice that fits, instead of being flattened by a generic brand.
